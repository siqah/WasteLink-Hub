About WasteLink Hub

WasteLink Hub is a platform designed to connect households and businesses with waste collectors and recycling centers to streamline waste management in Kenya.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce environmental pollution, promote recycling, and create economic opportunities within the waste management sector.

How It Works

1. Request a Pickup

Households and businesses can schedule a waste pickup at their convenience.

2. Collectors Accept

Registered collectors can view and accept pickup requests in their designated areas.

3. Waste is Recycled

Recyclable materials are transported to recycling centers, reducing landfill waste.
